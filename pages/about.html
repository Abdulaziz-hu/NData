Our Mission

N(DATA) exists to make technical specifications available to everyone, developers, researchers, engineers, and enthusiasts. No marketing fluff. No paywalls. No noise. No ads. Just clean, structured, machine-friendly data you can trust.

What We Provide

Huge Technical Database

Access specs for devices, hardware components, chips, displays, sensors, and more.

Open-Source

Completely open, community-maintained, MIT-licensed, and built for transparency.

No Clutter

Clean search, organized categories, and fast navigation for power users.

Structured Data

Every device spec is normalized, validated, and stored consistently.

Reliable & Transparent

All data sources are documented and manually reviewed for accuracy.

Multi-language

Available in English and Arabic, with RTL support and localized formatting.
